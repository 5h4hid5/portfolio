about me

intro

i live in dhaka, bangladesh. love to design and develop websites. in my passtime i am a blogger, reader, ambigram artist.

web development

i am a coder first. learnt java as academic requirement, but web technologies attract me most. coding for web for the last two years. mastered html5, css3, javascript and a little php. bootstrap is my favourite front-end framework. for heavy back-end application laravel is my first choice. if my project is a spa application without any confusion i will choose angularjs. i also love to work with wordpress.

ui/ux design

when i am not coding i am a ui/ux designer. i follow the minimalistic ideology, meaning i believe less is more. for spa or mobile app i follow the material design pattern. love to design portfolio site and mobile application most.

future plan

mastering nodejs is my next target and goal is to become a full-stack javascript developer. then i will try to convert some of my ideas into electron and ionic applications.
